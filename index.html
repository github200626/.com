Frequently asked questions

How much does Ramp charge in fees?

The fees we charge depend primarily on the payment or payout method you choose, and on the blockchain validation/mining costs at the time of the transaction. For a full breakdown of how our fees are structured, check out the fees we charge for buying and selling crypto.

Which countries and states does Ramp support?

Ramp is available in 150+ countries and territories and in 35 US states (plus the District of Columbia). You can find a list of all supported regions in this article.

How long does verification take?

The KYC verification process usually takes less than 10 minutes although in some cases it can take up to the following business day. The time frame to complete KYC verification may vary depending on the validity and completeness of your documentation and the number of requests being submitted. You can find more details in this article.

How long does it take to buy crypto?

The time it takes to receive crypto in your wallet once you initiate a purchase depends on the following factors: how long you take to make your payment, the payment method you choose, and the blockchain transaction processing time of your cryptoasset. You can find more details in this article.

How long does it take to sell crypto?

The total time it takes to sell crypto once you initiate a sale depends on the following factors: how long you take to transfer your crypto, how long until your transfer is processed and confirmed by the blockchain, and the payout method you choose.

Does Ramp offer solutions for business?

Yes, Ramp does offer solutions for businesses. Every business is unique and as such the solutions we offer will be based on the needs of the business. Please get in touch with our sales team if you have specific business inquiries.
